THE
JOURNEY

Piscis Aquarium was founded in 1995 by Mr. Pista and Mr. Cisto
to campaign, share, and educate people about the true
of ocean. Piscis Aquarium starts from Belgium and now has
expanded to more than 20 countries around the world to
provide information and education of ocean to people.

Aside from campaign and education, Piscis Aquarium also
doing research about Marine Animals and Life. Our Vision
is to educate people about the true essence of ocean and
develop new research of Marine Lifes to improve Marine
cultivation.
